Our Programs

Healthcare & Medical Care

Every child deserves access to timely, compassionate medical attention — no exceptions.

Program Overview

Health Is a Right, Not a Privilege

Many of the children who arrive at Light & Glory have never received proper medical attention. Our healthcare program ensures that this changes from day one. Every new child receives a full health assessment, and we maintain individual health records for each child in our care.

We facilitate regular medical check-ups in partnership with local healthcare facilities and visiting medical teams. From vaccinations and deworming to dental checks and eye tests, we ensure no aspect of a child's health is overlooked.

When illness strikes, children receive prompt treatment without delay. We maintain a first-aid kit and basic medical supplies on-site, and our staff are trained in basic healthcare and emergency response to act quickly when needed.

What We Provide

Our Medical Services

💉

Vaccinations

All children receive routine immunisations according to the Uganda national immunisation schedule.

🦷

Dental Care

Regular dental check-ups and basic oral hygiene education to protect children's teeth and gums.

👁️

Eye Screening

Vision tests help identify children who may need spectacles to see clearly in class and in life.

🩺

General Check-ups

Routine health assessments monitor growth, detect illness early, and track overall wellbeing.

🧪

Lab Testing

Blood tests and screenings for malaria, anaemia, and other common childhood conditions in Uganda.

🚑

Emergency Response

On-site first aid and rapid referral protocols ensure no child waits in a health emergency.

Why It Matters

A Healthy Child Thrives

  • 🔬
    Preventable Diseases Can Be Prevented

    In rural Uganda, children are particularly vulnerable to malaria, respiratory infections, and waterborne illness. Regular check-ups and preventive care dramatically reduce the risk of these conditions, keeping children healthy and in school.

  • ❤️
    Medical Care Restores Dignity

    For orphaned children who have lacked access to healthcare, receiving proper medical attention communicates something profound: you are valued, you matter, your body is worth caring for. That message is as healing as any medicine.

  • 📈
    Health and Education Are Linked

    A sick child cannot learn. By keeping children healthy, we protect their ability to attend school, concentrate in class, and build the academic foundation that will carry them into adulthood.
