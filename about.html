Our Story

How Light & Glory Began

Light & Glory Transformation Ministries was born out of one person's refusal to look away. In 2008, our founder walked through the villages of Namutumba and encountered children sleeping in the streets — malnourished, orphaned and without hope.

With nothing more than faith and a rented room, the home opened its doors to the first five children. What began as a single room is today a thriving children's home and primary school serving over 80 children.

"Every child that walks through our gate carries a wound. Our job is to make sure they leave carrying a dream."

— Founder, Light & Glory Transformation Ministries

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2012

The Home is Founded

Light & Glory opens its doors to the first five children in a rented room in Namutumba. The journey begins with faith and love.

2014

First Permanent Building

With donor support, the first permanent structure is built — a dormitory and dining hall for 25 children.

2016

Primary School Established

Light & Glory Primary School is registered and begins classes, ensuring every child gets quality education on site.

2017

First PLE Candidates

Our first Primary Seven class sits the national PLE examinations. All pupils pass — two achieve First Grade.

2019

Medical Clinic Opened

A basic clinic is established on the grounds, providing free healthcare to children and vulnerable families nearby.

2022

80 Children in Care

The home reaches a milestone — 80 children in full-time care, with 30+ alumni now in secondary school and beyond.

2024

New Classroom Block

A new four-classroom block is completed, giving children modern learning spaces for years to come.
