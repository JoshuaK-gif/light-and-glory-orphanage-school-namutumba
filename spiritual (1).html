Our Programs

Faith & Spiritual Growth

Rooting every child in Christian values, character, and a deep sense of God-given identity.

Program Overview

Growing in Faith & Character

At Light & Glory, we believe that true transformation begins on the inside. Our spiritual growth program is woven into the very fabric of daily life at the orphanage — it is not an add-on, but the foundation upon which everything else is built.

Each day begins with morning devotions — a time of prayer, Scripture reading, and worship that centres the children on God's love and faithfulness. Weekly chapel services, Bible study groups, and discipleship mentoring deepen each child's personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

Character development is equally central. We teach children the values of honesty, kindness, respect, service, and resilience — equipping them to become not just educated individuals, but upright and compassionate members of society. Our staff lead by example, modelling the faith they preach.

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it." — Proverbs 22:6
Why It Matters

Identity, Purpose, Hope

  • ✨
    Every Child Needs to Know They Are Loved

    Orphaned children often carry deep feelings of rejection, worthlessness, and abandonment. Teaching them that they are created in God's image — deeply loved, chosen, and purposeful — is one of the most powerful and healing truths we can offer.

  • 🕊️
    Faith Builds Resilience

    Life will bring challenges. Children grounded in faith have an anchor that holds them through difficulty, failure, and uncertainty. The spiritual formation we provide gives our children the inner resources to face whatever lies ahead with courage and hope.

  • 🌟
    Character Is the True Measure of Success

    We do not measure our success by academic grades alone. Our deepest goal is to raise godly, generous, and morally upright young people who will serve their communities, lead with integrity, and be a light to Uganda and the world.
