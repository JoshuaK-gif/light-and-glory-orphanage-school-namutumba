Our Programs

Education & Mentorship

Equipping every child with knowledge, confidence, and a future full of possibility.

Program Overview

Quality Education for Every Child

At Light & Glory Orphanage School, we believe that education is the single most powerful gift we can give an orphaned child. Our primary school program follows the Uganda National Curriculum, ensuring every child is prepared for national examinations and future academic success.

Beyond textbooks, our dedicated teachers serve as mentors — providing emotional support, encouragement, and a safe space where children are free to ask questions, make mistakes, and grow. Each child receives individual attention to ensure no one is left behind.

We supplement classroom learning with reading clubs, mathematics support groups, and creative arts — nurturing the whole child, not just their academic performance.

Why It Matters

The Power of Learning

  • 🎓
    Breaking the Cycle of Poverty

    For orphaned children in rural Namutumba District, access to quality education is not guaranteed. Without it, children face a future of limited options. Our school creates a path out of poverty through structured learning and academic achievement.

  • 💛
    Healing Through Learning

    Many of our children carry deep emotional wounds from loss and hardship. A structured, caring school environment gives them stability, routine, and a sense of belonging — all of which are essential to psychological healing and healthy development.

  • 🌱
    Mentorship That Lasts a Lifetime

    Our teachers do more than teach — they mentor. By investing in each child's unique strengths and dreams, we build the self-confidence and resilience that will carry these children through every challenge life presents.
