Our Programs

Meals & Nutrition

Ensuring every child starts each day nourished, healthy, and ready to learn.

Program Overview

No Child Goes to Bed Hungry

Proper nutrition is the foundation upon which everything else at Light & Glory is built. A hungry child cannot focus in class, cannot play freely, and cannot grow into their full potential. Our nutrition program guarantees that every child in our care receives three balanced, hot meals every single day of the year.

Our kitchen staff prepare meals rich in locally grown foods — matoke, beans, groundnuts, leafy greens, and seasonal fruits — reflecting the beauty of Uganda's agricultural abundance. We work with local farmers to source fresh produce, supporting the surrounding community as we feed our children.

We also track each child's growth and nutritional health in partnership with visiting healthcare workers, identifying any deficiencies early and adjusting meals accordingly.

Why It Matters

Food Is Foundation

  • 🧠
    Nutrition Fuels Learning

    Research consistently shows that well-nourished children have better concentration, memory, and academic performance. By feeding our children properly, we are directly investing in their educational outcomes and future opportunities.

  • 💪
    Strong Bodies, Strong Futures

    Malnutrition in childhood leads to stunted physical and cognitive development with lifelong consequences. Our daily meal program prevents malnutrition and builds the physical strength children need to grow, play, and thrive.

  • 🏡
    Mealtime Creates Community

    Sharing meals is one of the most powerful ways we build family culture at the orphanage. Children gather together, give thanks, and develop a sense of belonging — transforming a simple meal into a moment of love, safety, and togetherness.

How You Can Help

Feed a Child, Change a Life

A small donation can provide nutritious meals for a child for an entire week. Your generosity means no child at Light & Glory will ever face hunger. Every meal you fund is a step toward a healthier, brighter future.
