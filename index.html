Life at Light & Glory

A Day Full of Love, Learning & Laughter

Every morning begins with devotion and praise. The children gather together, sing, pray, and start the day with joy and gratitude in their hearts.

By 8am, classrooms are full of energy. Children are reading, writing, and discovering the world many for the first time in a safe and caring environment.

At lunchtime, hot meals are shared together like one big family. In the afternoon, the compound comes alive with football, games, and the sound of children simply being children.

At the end of every day, each child goes to bed knowing they are loved, safe, and valued. That is what your support makes possible.

What We Do

Every day at Light & Glory, lives are being changed. Here is how.

Education

At Light and Glory Orphanage School, we provide quality primary education to orphaned and underprivileged children, giving them the foundation they need to succeed. Our programs go beyond the classroom offering structured learning, mentorship, and emotional support that nurture the whole child.

We believe that every child, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves the chance to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. Through dedicated teachers and a nurturing environment, we walk with each child on their journey toward a brighter future.

Meals & Nutrition

At Light and Glory Orphanage School, we believe that no child can learn on an empty stomach. That is why every child in our care receives a hot, nutritious meal every day prepared with care and designed to meet their growing needs.

A fed child is a focused child. By ensuring every student is well-nourished, we create the conditions for real learning to happen where young minds are alert, energized, and ready to absorb, grow, and thrive.

Medical Care

At Light and Glory Orphanage School, we understand that a healthy child is a thriving child. That is why we ensure every child in our care has access to basic healthcare, regular medical checkups, and prompt treatment whenever they fall sick because good health is the foundation of everything else.

From routine wellness checks to handling medical emergencies, we walk alongside each child with the care and attention they deserve. No child should have to face illness alone, and at Light and Glory, none of them ever do.

Spiritual Growth

At Light and Glory Orphanage School, faith is at the heart of everything we do. Rooted in Christian values, we believe that nurturing a child's spiritual foundation is just as important as their academic growth. Through daily devotion, worship, and prayer, we create an environment where children grow not just in knowledge, but in character and faith.

We are committed to raising young people of integrity, compassion, and purpose. By grounding each child in the values of love, honesty, and service, we equip them to face the world with confidence and a heart anchored in something greater than themselves.

Vocational Skills

As our children grow, so does our commitment to their future. At Light and Glory Orphanage School, we equip older children with practical, hands-on skills that prepare them for life beyond our walls empowering them to stand on their own feet and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

We believe that true care goes beyond providing shelter and education it means setting every young person up for independence and dignity. By investing in their abilities and potential, we raise not just survivors, but confident, self-reliant, and productive members of society.

Safe Haven

Nestled in the heart of Namutumba District, Uganda, Light and Glory Orphanage School is more than an institution it is a home. We open our doors to orphaned and vulnerable children, offering them a safe, warm, and loving environment where they are seen, valued, and cared for.

Every child who comes to us carries their own story. We meet them where they are, wrapping them in stability, compassion, and belonging. Here, they are not just students they are family, and we are committed to walking with them every step of the way.
