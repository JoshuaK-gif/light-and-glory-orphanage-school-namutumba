Life at Light & Glory

A Day Full of Love, Learning & Laughter

Every morning begins with devotion and praise. The children gather together, sing, pray, and start the day with joy and gratitude in their hearts.

By 8am, classrooms are full of energy. Children are reading, writing, and discovering the world many for the first time in a safe and caring environment.

At lunchtime, hot meals are shared together like one big family. In the afternoon, the compound comes alive with football, games, and the sound of children simply being children.

At the end of every day, each child goes to bed knowing they are loved, safe, and valued. That is what your support makes possible.

Our Mission

To be hope to those without help through generosity, empower and train leaders for generation to come; preach the Gospel of Our Lord Jesus Christ to all.

What We Do

Every day at Light & Glory, lives are being changed. Here is how.

Education

We provide quality primary education to orphaned and underprivileged children, giving them the foundation they need to succeed.

Meals & Nutrition

Every child receives hot, nutritious meals daily. A fed child is a focused child — ready to learn and grow.

Medical Care

We ensure every child has access to basic healthcare, medical checkups, and treatment when they are sick.

Spiritual Growth

Rooted in Christian values, we nurture each child's faith and character through daily devotion and worship.

Vocational Skills

Older children are equipped with practical skills to help them become self-reliant and productive members of society.

Safe Haven

We provide a safe, loving home for orphans and vulnerable children in Namutumba District, Uganda.
